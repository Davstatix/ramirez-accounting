## Questions to Consider

1. **Business Structure**: What business entity type are you using? (Sole Proprietor, LLC, etc.)
2. **Insurance**: Have you gotten quotes for professional liability insurance?
3. **Legal**: Do you have an attorney to review your contracts?
4. **Credentials**: Are there any state requirements for bookkeeping services in your state?
5. **Pricing**: Are you confident in your pricing structure?
6. **Capacity**: How many clients can you handle initially?
7. **Software**: Are you comfortable with QuickBooks and other tools you'll use?
8. **Marketing**: How will you get your first clients?
